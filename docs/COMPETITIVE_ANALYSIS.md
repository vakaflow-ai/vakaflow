# Competitive Analysis: Agent Onboarding/Offboarding Platform

## Executive Summary

The market for agent onboarding/offboarding platforms is **fragmented and evolving**, with most solutions focused on **employee/user lifecycle management** or **vendor onboarding** rather than **AI agent lifecycle management**. The specific application of **RAG-based agent onboarding for AI agents** appears to be an **emerging niche** with limited direct competition, presenting a significant market opportunity.

### Market Segmentation

1. **Employee/User Onboarding/Offboarding** (Mature Market)

   - High competition, established players
   - Limited relevance to AI agent management

2. **Vendor/Supplier Onboarding** (Growing Market)
