## Target Buyer Analysis

### Market Segmentation

#### Primary Market: Large Enterprises

**Characteristics**:
- **Company Size**: 1,000+ employees
- **Revenue**: $100M+ annually
- **Agent Volume**: 50+ vendor agents
- **Compliance Requirements**: High (regulated industries)
- **IT Budget**: $10M+ annually
- **Decision-Making**: Complex, multi-stakeholder

**Industries**:
- Financial Services (banks, insurance, fintech)
- Healthcare (hospitals, health systems)
- Technology (enterprise SaaS, cloud providers)
- Retail/E-commerce (large retailers)

**Geographic Focus**:
- North America: 60% of target market
- Europe: 30% of target market
- Asia-Pacific: 10% of target market

#### Secondary Market: Mid-Market Companies

**Characteristics**:
- **Company Size**: 200-1,000 employees
- **Revenue**: $20M-$100M annually
- **Agent Volume**: 10-50 vendor agents
- **Compliance Requirements**: Medium
- **IT Budget**: $2M-$10M annually
- **Decision-Making**: Faster, fewer stakeholders

**Industries**:
- Mid-market financial services
- Regional healthcare providers
- Growing tech companies
- Manufacturing companies

## Buyer Personas

### Persona 1: Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)

**Demographics**:
- **Age**: 40-55
- **Education**: Bachelor's/Master's in CS, CISSP/CISM certified
- **Experience**: 15+ years in security
- **Location**: US, Europe

**Role & Responsibilities**:
- Overall security strategy
- Risk management
- Compliance oversight
- Vendor security assessments
- Security incident response

**Goals**:
- Reduce security risks from vendor agents
- Ensure compliance with regulations
- Streamline security reviews
- Maintain audit readiness
- Protect company data and reputation

**Pain Points**:
- Manual security reviews are time-consuming
- Lack of visibility into agent security posture
- Compliance gaps discovered late
- Difficulty tracking security across agents
- Resource constraints

**Challenges**:
- Balancing security with business speed
- Keeping up with evolving threats
- Managing multiple compliance frameworks
- Demonstrating ROI of security investments

**Information Sources**:
- Security conferences (RSA, Black Hat)
- Security publications (Dark Reading, CSO)
- Peer networks (CISO forums)
- Industry reports (Gartner, Forrester)

**Buying Process**:
- **Discovery**: Attends conferences, reads reports
- **Evaluation**: Reviews security features, compliance capabilities
- **Decision**: Involves legal, compliance, IT teams
- **Purchase**: Requires executive approval

**Messaging**:
- "Reduce security review time by 70%"
- "Automated compliance checking"
- "Enterprise-grade security"
- "Audit-ready documentation"

**Influence**: High (security approval required)
