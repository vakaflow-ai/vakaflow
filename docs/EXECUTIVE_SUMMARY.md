# Executive Summary: Agent Onboarding/Offboarding Platform

## The Opportunity

**Market Gap**: No comprehensive platform exists for AI agent lifecycle management with RAG-powered compliance checking.

**Market Size**: 
- North America: 38% of global market (2024)
- Growing rapidly with enterprise AI adoption
- Low direct competition in AI agent lifecycle management

**Target Market**: 
- Large enterprises with 50+ vendor agents
- Industries: Financial Services, Healthcare, Technology, E-commerce
- 200+ target customers by Year 3

## The Solution

**Platform**: RAG-powered AI agent onboarding/offboarding platform

**Key Features**:
- Automated compliance checking using RAG
- AI-powered recommendations
- Multi-stage review workflows
- Complete lifecycle management
- Knowledge preservation during offboarding

**Differentiation**:
- Specialized for AI agents (not generic onboarding)
- Advanced RAG capabilities (not rule-based)
- Complete lifecycle (not point solutions)
- Proven ROI (1,000%+)
